## Story

**As a** marketing strategist,  
**I want** the approved strategy to generate copywriting, lead magnets, funnel assets, and captation-ready materials,  
**so that** marketing and lead-generation teams can begin execution from approved assets instead of creating every landing page, CTA, and nurture sequence manually.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Each priority campaign can produce the source-required copywriting set: headlines, CTAs, body copy, and associated campaign copy variants.
2. The module generates 5-10 lead magnets aligned to persona and strategy context, with funnel stage assignment.
3. Each lead magnet includes a landing-page content package at minimum covering hero, benefits, social proof, and CTA.
4. Each lead magnet includes a 5-7 email nurture sequence as described in the concept.
5. The module exposes a captation-ready artifact pack that supports the source concept of turning strategy into lead-generation assets, without inventing undocumented cross-system automation.
6. UI and data structures no longer reduce copy and lead magnets to placeholder rows or absent modules.
