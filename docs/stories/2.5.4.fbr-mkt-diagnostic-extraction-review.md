## Story

**As a** marketing strategist,  
**I want** the system to extract a complete diagnostic and let me review it before strategy generation,  
**so that** SWOT, persona, UVP, viability score, niche, and business context are validated by a human before downstream outputs are generated.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. The extraction flow produces a diagnostic containing SWOT, persona, UVP, viability score, niche, and budget/timeline estimation as described in the concept and PRD.
2. The diagnostic review page exists and exposes a 4-quadrant SWOT matrix, a full persona card, UVP display, and viability score.
3. Extracted fields are editable by the strategist before approval, and edits are persisted without requiring regeneration from zero.
4. Diagnostic approval/rejection is represented explicitly in persistence and governs whether strategy generation can proceed.
5. UI and API behavior no longer reduce diagnostic extraction to placeholder text or static labels.
