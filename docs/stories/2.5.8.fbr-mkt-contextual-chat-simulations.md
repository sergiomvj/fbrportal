## Story

**As a** marketing strategist,  
**I want** a contextual assistant attached to the active strategy,  
**so that** I can refine strategy, copy, calendar, and budget scenarios without restarting the workflow or losing version context.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. The MKT chat is scoped to the active strategy/project and includes the context described by the source artifacts: diagnostic, current version, copy, calendar, and recent chat history.
2. The chat supports persisted history and paginated retrieval instead of transient UI-only messages, including the source-expected context-window behavior for model assembly.
3. Long assistant responses support source-approved streaming semantics.
4. The assistant exposes proactive next-action suggestions and inconsistency flags as described by the concept, not only reactive free-text replies.
5. The assistant can drive scenario/refinement operations relevant to the MKT concept, such as tone adjustment, budget redistribution, date refinement, and strategy-parameter tuning, without bypassing persistence/version rules.
6. Chat UX is available from the strategy workflow surfaces rather than being absent from the product.
