## Story

**As a** finance manager,
**I want** audit visibility, DRE reports, exports, and anomaly alerts,
**so that** finance governance can be reviewed, shared, and acted on quickly.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. `GET /api/proxy/finance/audit` exists with role checks and pagination.
2. DRE report endpoint exists.
3. Async CSV/PDF export endpoint exists with role checks, status handling, and expiration/error states.
4. Audit log page is restricted to Gestor, Financeiro, and Auditor and includes table columns, diff viewer, filters, CSV export, empty state, and local timestamp formatting.
5. Reports page includes type/format/company/period/DMPL form, generation polling, report history, CSV/PDF download, PDF preview where possible, and expired/error states.
6. Anomaly detection worker/routes create portal notifications for critical alerts and support alert list, resolve action, and unresolved badge.
