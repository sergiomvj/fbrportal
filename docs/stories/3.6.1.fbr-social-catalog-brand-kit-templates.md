# Story 3.6.1: FBR-Social Catalog, Brand-Kit Sync, and Templates

## Status

Complete ✅ (2026-05-14)

## Story

**As a** social production operator,  
**I want** Social to maintain network formats, safe zones, brand-kit sync, and templates,  
**so that** every social artifact starts from valid production constraints.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Catalog includes the PRD-required social networks and formats.
2. Each format stores dimensions, safe zone, extension, and size constraints.
3. Templates support CRUD/listing and versioning.
4. Brand-kit updates from Design are received through a tested proxy/webhook.
5. UI/API uses catalog and template data rather than hardcoded placeholders.
6. Tests cover catalog, safe-zone rules, template versions, and Design brand-kit sync.
