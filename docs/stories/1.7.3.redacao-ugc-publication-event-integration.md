## Story

**As a** newsroom operator,  
**I want** UGC moderation, alert handling, publication behavior, and downstream editorial events to be explicit and reliable,  
**so that** community submissions and published articles move through controlled integrations.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. The current repo exposes UGC and alert flows through the portal proxy/store implementation.
2. The story records exactly which source-doc responsibilities are partially simulated today and which are still missing.
3. WordPress publication and `article.published` downstream integration are not marked complete until implemented.
4. Remaining integration work is captured for future delivery.

## Dev Notes

### What Exists Now

- `apps/portal/src/app/api/proxy/redacao/ugc/route.ts`
  - Lists UGC, creates UGC, accepts UGC, and rejects UGC through the store.
- `apps/portal/src/app/api/proxy/redacao/alertas/route.ts`
  - Lists alerts, resolves one alert, and resolves all alerts.
- `apps/portal/src/lib/redacao/store.ts`
  - Creates an article with `etapa='coletado'` when UGC is accepted.
  - Stores alert taxonomy and alert resolution metadata.

### What Is Still Missing

- No duplicate-detection rule for repeated UGC submissions in 24h.
- No persisted credits ledger.
- No real publication service to WordPress.
- No outbound `article.published` event wiring to FBR-Sales or another consumer.
- No notification service or retry queue for critical alerts.
