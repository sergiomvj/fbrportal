## Story

**As a** finance manager,
**I want** a Finance landing surface with module navigation, KPIs, Arva agent slots, integrations, and governance visibility,
**so that** the finance team can understand the operating model before entering detailed workflows.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Finance visual tokens are available and applied: `--finance: #10B981`, `--finance-glow: rgba(16,185,129,0.18)`, and `--blue: #3B82F6`.
2. The Finance page renders hero KPIs: `100% integrados`, `N empresas`, `0 transacoes sem rastreio`, and `24h SLA`.
3. The page renders 6 module cards for Recebimentos, Pagamentos, Centro de Custo, Conciliacao, Forecasting, and Auditoria, each with icon, title, description, and link.
4. The page renders 7 finance agent slots: Vault, Intake, Payout, Ledger, Reconcile, Oracle, and Sentinel.
5. The page includes the integration table for FBR-Sales, Click, Dev, Suporte, Leads, Players Externos, and Bancos/Fintechs.
6. The page includes a governance section with approval limits and roles CFO, Gestor, Analista, Auditor, and Owner.
7. The "Incluir Agente" entry point uses the shared Arva/AgentPicker contract filtered by tags `financeiro` and `contabilidade`.
