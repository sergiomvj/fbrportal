# Story 3.5.2: FBR-Design Job Pipeline, Render, Review, and Gallery

## Status

Complete ✅ (2026-05-14)

## Story

**As a** creative production lead,  
**I want** Design jobs to move from briefing through render, review, and gallery delivery,  
**so that** approved creative assets are produced as real artifacts.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Job queue persists briefing, selected brand kit, status, variant, and delivery metadata.
2. Pipeline stages cover briefing, lookup, asset finder, composition, auto-review, render, and delivery.
3. Rendered assets are stored and visible in the gallery.
4. Variant selection is persisted per job.
5. Review status and rejection reasons are persisted and visible.
6. Tests cover job lifecycle, render artifact metadata, review, and gallery listing.
