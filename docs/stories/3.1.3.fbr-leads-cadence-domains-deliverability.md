# Story 3.1.3: FBR-Leads Cadence, Domains, and Deliverability

## Status

Complete ✅ (2026-05-14)

## Story

**As a** growth operator,  
**I want** outbound domains, templates, and 4-touch cadences governed by deliverability health,  
**so that** FBR-Leads can run outreach without unsafe or unobservable sending behavior.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Domains have persisted health, warmup, bounce, pause, and ownership state.
2. Email templates are CRUD-capable and company-scoped.
3. Cadences support the PRD-required 4 touches with timing, template, and stop conditions.
4. Deliverability rules can pause a domain or cadence automatically when thresholds are exceeded.
5. Cadence execution is represented through jobs/workflows or an approved repository equivalent.
6. UI/API exposes domain health, cadence status, and failure reasons.
7. Tests cover cadence scheduling, domain health transitions, bounce handling, and pause behavior.
