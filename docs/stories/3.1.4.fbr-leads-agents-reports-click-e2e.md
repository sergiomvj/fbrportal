# Story 3.1.4: FBR-Leads Agents, Reports, and Click End-to-End Handoff

## Status

Complete ✅ (2026-05-14)

## Story

**As a** revenue operations owner,  
**I want** Leads agents, reports, and the qualified-lead handoff to work as one observable flow,  
**so that** Click receives only qualified leads with complete context and audit evidence.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Leads agent surfaces reflect real capture, scoring, cadence, and handoff execution state.
2. Operational reports summarize capture, scoring, cadence health, qualification, and handoff outcomes.
3. `lead.qualified` payload includes identification, contact, qualification, enrichment, cadence, history, deduplication, priority, and suggested action.
4. Handoff is idempotent and observable in both Leads and Click.
5. The full flow is tested from captured lead through Click deal creation.
6. Evidence updates both Leads and Click conformity stories when cross-module contracts are touched.
