# Story 3.1.1: FBR-Leads Data Foundation and Source Capture

## Status

Complete ✅ (2026-05-14)

## Story

**As a** growth operator,  
**I want** FBR-Leads to persist ICPs, leads, source runs, and captured records from the PRD-required sources,  
**so that** outbound work starts from real auditable lead data instead of static or fixture-only records.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Leads, ICPs, source runs, source records, domains, templates, and cadence-related entities have repository-approved persistence contracts.
2. RLS or equivalent company isolation prevents one company from reading or mutating another company's lead data.
3. Source capture supports the PRD-required source types: LinkedIn, CNPJ.biz, Google Maps, and website scraping.
4. Every captured lead records source, timestamp, normalized contact/company fields, enrichment metadata, and duplicate status.
5. Capture runs expose terminal states: pending, processing, done, failed.
6. API/proxy routes never bypass the approved backend/proxy boundary.
7. Tests cover persistence shape, company isolation, source normalization, deduplication, and failure state.
