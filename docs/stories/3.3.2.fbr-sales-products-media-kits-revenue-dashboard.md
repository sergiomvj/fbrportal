# Story 3.3.2: FBR-Sales Products, Media Kits, Revenue, and Dashboard

## Status

Complete ✅ (2026-05-14)

## Story

**As a** commercial manager,  
**I want** Sales products, media kits, revenue cards, tables, and KPIs to use real module data,  
**so that** the department can operate after a deal closes.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Espacos/produtos comerciais have CRUD or approved read/write contracts.
2. Media kits are persisted, linked to products/partners, and exposed through API/UI.
3. Revenue cards and tables derive from persisted partner/revenue data.
4. Sales dashboard KPIs derive from real Sales state.
5. Revenue state remains compatible with `Sales -> Finance` forwarding.
6. Tests cover products, media kits, revenue aggregation, and dashboard KPIs.
