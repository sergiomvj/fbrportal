## Story

**As a** marketing strategist,  
**I want** the approved strategy to become a 90-day calendar and execution roadmap,  
**so that** the team can move from diagnosis and planning into scheduled work, quick wins, and phased operational delivery.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. The module generates a 90-day calendar aligned to strategy and niche context with organic-vs-paid distinction.
2. Quick Wins are explicitly marked for the first 30 days as described in the source concept.
3. The roadmap view exists and groups execution tasks by phase/time window with checklist-style outputs.
4. Calendar data supports at minimum date, channel, type, theme, copy summary, and publication status.
5. Calendar export to CSV exists with the source-required editorial headers for downstream management tools.
6. Roadmap data supports phase, owner/responsible party, deadline, tool/context notes, and alert-ready metadata required by the concept.
7. Calendar and roadmap are no longer represented by hardcoded placeholder rows.
