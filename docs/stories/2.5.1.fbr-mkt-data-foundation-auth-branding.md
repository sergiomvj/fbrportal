## Story

**As a** platform owner,  
**I want** FBR-MKT to start from a source-accurate data, auth, branding, and tenant-isolation foundation,  
**so that** every downstream MKT workflow can persist diagnostics, strategies, chat, exports, and agent activity without mock-only behavior or cross-company leakage.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. The approved MKT persistence surface exists in the current repository for the 16 entities required by `TASKS_MKT.md`: `organizations`, `users`, `projects`, `documents`, `extracted_data`, `strategies`, `campaigns`, `copywriting_variants`, `calendar_items`, `lead_magnets`, `roadmap_tasks`, `chat_messages`, `exports`, `agents`, `agent_action_logs`, and `processing_jobs`.
2. Every table containing organization or user data enforces tenant isolation/RLS so company A cannot read or mutate company B MKT data.
3. Branding-by-organization exists for logo, colors, and export branding inputs required by the concept before PDF/PPTX generation is enabled.
4. Auth and roles support at minimum `Admin`, `Editor`, and `Viewer`, and role checks are available to MKT proxy routes and UI surfaces.
5. `agent_action_logs` is append-only and preserves immutable evidence of who generated, approved, exported, or changed MKT artifacts.
6. `processing_jobs` can represent at minimum `upload`, `extracao`, `geracao_estrategia`, `copy`, `calendario`, and `export` job categories with observable status and timestamps.
7. The story removes in-memory-only MKT persistence as the source of truth for production paths covered by this story.
