## Story

**As a** marketing strategist,  
**I want** an approved diagnostic to generate a full strategy master with immutable versions and campaign briefings,  
**so that** positioning, channel mix, KPIs, and prioritized campaigns become a real working plan instead of mock records.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Approved diagnostics can trigger strategy generation that produces positioning, brand archetype, tone of voice, UVP framing, channel mix with justification, KPIs by channel, and at least 3 prioritized campaigns.
2. Every generation or regeneration creates an immutable strategy snapshot/version instead of mutating the previous approved version in place.
3. The Strategy Master page exists and exposes the generated strategy in structured sections rather than a flat campaigns table.
4. Campaign briefings include the source-required fields such as SMART objective, key messages, budget, timeline, formats, and segmented audiences.
5. Budget redistribution or scenario-adjustment support is represented in the strategy domain and can be surfaced by downstream chat/export stories.
