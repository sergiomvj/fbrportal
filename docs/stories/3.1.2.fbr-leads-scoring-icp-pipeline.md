# Story 3.1.2: FBR-Leads Scoring, ICP Matching, and Pipeline

## Status

Partial ⚠️ (2026-05-14) - ICP CRUD e 7 stages implementados, cálculo de score emfixo em fixtures

## Story

**As a** sales development lead,  
**I want** captured leads scored, matched to ICPs, and moved through the PRD pipeline,  
**so that** qualification is explainable and ready for handoff to Click.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. The scoring algorithm implements the PRD-required 12 variables with bonus and penalty rules.
2. Every score stores a breakdown explaining each variable and adjustment.
3. ICP matching compares lead attributes against persisted ICP definitions.
4. Pipeline stages follow the PRD-required 7-stage lifecycle.
5. Stage changes are validated, auditable, and company-scoped.
6. Dashboard KPIs and funil visual derive from real persisted lead/pipeline data.
7. Tests cover scoring, ICP match, stage transitions, and dashboard aggregation.
