# Story 3.7.2: FBR-Finance Reconciliation Worker and Human Pendencies

## Status

Complete ✅ (2026-05-14)

## Story

**As a** finance analyst,  
**I want** asynchronous reconciliation with human pendencies and divergence rules,  
**so that** financial records can be matched safely without manual spreadsheet work.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Reconciliation worker/runtime decision is recorded and implemented.
2. Reconciliation jobs have pending, processing, done, failed states and retry/failure visibility.
3. Divergence rules generate human pendencies when automatic matching is unsafe.
4. Pendencies support approve, reject, and create-entry flows.
5. Duplicate statements and already reconciled items are handled safely.
6. Tests cover worker state, divergence rules, pendency lifecycle, duplicate handling, and failure state.
