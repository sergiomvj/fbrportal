## Story

**As a** finance manager,
**I want** Finance records modeled with company isolation, approval structures, and immutable audit history,
**so that** all financial data can be traced and governed across group companies.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Migration coverage exists for all 14 Finance tables: `companies`, `cost_centers`, `transactions`, `receivables`, `payables`, `bank_accounts`, `bank_statements`, `reconciliation_items`, `budgets`, `suppliers`, `approval_flows`, `forecasts`, `audit_log`, and `agent_actions`.
2. SPEC baseline tables/semantics for `empresas_financeiras`, `recebimentos`, `pagamentos`, and `audit_financeiro` are either represented directly or explicitly mapped to the English table names.
3. Finance tables enforce company isolation/RLS so users from company A cannot read or mutate company B records.
4. Audit records are append-only; direct update/delete attempts fail outside approved trigger/service boundaries.
5. Receivables/payables mutations create audit records preserving `valor_antes` and `valor_depois`.
6. SQL-backed monetary values use `NUMERIC(15,2)`, timestamps use `TIMESTAMPTZ`, before/after payloads use `JSONB`, and identifiers use UUIDs.
