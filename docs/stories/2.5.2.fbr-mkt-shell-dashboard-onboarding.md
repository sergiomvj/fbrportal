## Story

**As a** first-time MKT user,  
**I want** the FBR-MKT shell to reflect the original product language and onboarding flow,  
**so that** I understand the full path from company input to exported strategy instead of seeing a generic campaign dashboard.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. The MKT landing route uses the approved visual tokens from `TASKS_MKT.md`: `--mkt`, `--mkt-dim`, and `--mkt-faint`.
2. The main hero reflects the source concept and shows the 8 KPI highlights required by `MK-V02`.
3. The shell exposes the 9 module cards from `MK-V03`: Auth, Diagnóstico, Extração, Estratégia, Campanhas, Calendário, Lead Magnets, Roadmap, and Outputs.
4. The shell includes the 7-pillar visualization from `MK-V04`: Brand Identity, Target Audience, Channel Strategy, Conversion Funnel, KPIs, Editorial Calendar, and Lead Magnets.
5. The shell includes the 6 MKT agent cards from `MK-V05` and the 6-card service loop from `MK-V06`.
6. The onboarding flow reflects the concept goal of taking the user from first access to first export, including the source step sequence up to export.
7. This story removes copy and layout that position MKT primarily as a generic campaigns module.
